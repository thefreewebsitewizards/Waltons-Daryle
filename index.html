Our Journey

From First Trip to Wondrous Adventures

Walton’s Wondrous Adventures is built around one idea: your trip should feel effortless, personal, and unforgettable. We plan with intention, recommend with honesty, and handle the details so you can focus on the experience.

My name is Daryle Walton. I am a travel agent, as well as a licensed insurance agent.

I worked hard to graduate college. I worked hard to serve this great nation’s military. I will work hard for you.

The first thing you notice is the light.

Maybe it’s the golden sunrise stretching across the turquoise waters of Hawaii. Or the crisp, glacier-kissed air in Alaska as a bald eagle glides overhead. Perhaps it’s the quiet magic of drifting through the canals of Venice, where history whispers from every stone.

Travel has a way of awakening something in us.

It begins as a simple idea — a beach escape, a family adventure, a long-awaited honeymoon — but it becomes so much more. It becomes the laughter shared on a sunset cruise departing from Miami. It becomes toes in the sand in Cancún, where the sea seems to stretch endlessly into possibility. It becomes standing in awe beneath the ancient glow of the Colosseum, imagining the stories carried through centuries.

Travel changes us.

It reconnects families.
It celebrates love.
It turns milestones into memories.

For some, it’s the thrill of sailing across open waters with world-class dining and entertainment. For others, it’s an all-inclusive retreat where the only decision is whether to relax by the pool or walk along the shoreline at sunset. And for couples beginning a new chapter, it’s the romance of discovering somewhere new together — hand in hand, hearts wide open.

The beauty of travel isn’t just in the destination.

It’s in the anticipation. The planning. The dream taking shape.

Every journey tells a story — of discovery, of connection, of wonder. From the vibrant streets of Paris to the lush rainforests of Costa Rica, each experience adds a new chapter to your personal adventure.

And when you return home, suitcase a little heavier and heart completely full, you realize something important:

You didn’t just take a trip.
You created a memory that will last a lifetime.

Where will your story begin?

Travel Tips

Essential Tips for a Smoother Journey

Here are 15 essential travel tips to enhance your journey. As it is not possible for me to cover every situation or even be all-inclusive, it would be wise to “Google” you own specific situation and prepare wisely.

Pack Smart

  • Use a small backpack or suitcase to limit what you carry.
  • Always pack a towel; it’s versatile for various situations.
  • Write a packing list, then cut it in half to avoid overpacking.
  • Bring extra socks to account for laundry mishaps and wear.

Plan Ahead

  • Set a budget and stick to it to avoid overspending.
  • Research off-season travel deals to save money.
  • Book accommodations and transport in advance for better rates.

Stay Safe

  • Keep copies of important documents in case of loss.
  • Use a money belt or hidden pouch for valuables.

Enjoy the Experience

  • Embrace local culture and try new foods.
  • Stay flexible with your itinerary to explore unexpected opportunities.

These tips can help make your travels smoother and more enjoyable, whether you're a seasoned traveler or a novice.
